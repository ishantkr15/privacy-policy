Privacy Policy

Your goals, your data, your privacy — always and absolutely.

verified_user

At a glance: GoalBridge operates entirely on your device. No account required. No cloud storage. No data collection. Your personal information — goals, milestones, reflections, progress — never leaves your phone. Period.

handshake Our Privacy Commitment

GoalBridge was built with a radical belief: your personal ambitions and reflections are yours alone. We don't want them, we don't see them, and we've architected the app so we can't access them even if we wanted to.

This isn't a marketing promise — it's a technical guarantee baked into the architecture.

search_off Information We Collect

cancel We DO NOT collect:

  • Personal identification (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Usage analytics or behavioral tracking
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Your goals, tasks, reflections, or progress data

check_circle What stays on your device:

  • All goals, milestones, and tasks you create
  • Personal reflections and journal entries
  • Goal images you attach (optional)
  • Progress tracking data and streaks
  • App preferences and settings

database Data Storage & Architecture

All GoalBridge data is stored locally using Room, Android's encrypted SQLite database framework. The application does not transmit any data over the internet. There are:

  • No cloud servers
  • No synchronization services
  • No third-party data processors
  • No analytics SDKs
  • No advertising frameworks

backup Backup & Export

GoalBridge includes optional local backup functionality. You can export your data as a JSON or text file saved directly to your device. This file remains entirely under your control and is never uploaded to any server.

settings_accessibility Permissions We Request

  • Notifications: For task reminders, milestone alerts, and weekly reflection prompts. All notifications are generated locally. You can disable them anytime in Settings.
  • Photo Library Access (Optional): Only if you choose to attach images to your goals. Images are stored locally and never shared.
  • Vibration: For haptic feedback on interactive elements. Can be disabled in system settings.

child_care Children's Privacy

GoalBridge does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The app is safe for all ages as it operates entirely offline with absolutely no data collection, no social features, and no external communications of any kind.
